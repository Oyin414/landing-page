Simple study tips to help make a difference now!
Prioritise getting a good night's rest every night so that your brain is fully rested the next day and ready for a helpful revision session.
Make sure to focus and engage in lessons at school amd if you are unsure about something , make sure to ask a teacher for help.
Group study sessions are an excellent way to help with retaining information, they make it so that if you're stuck you can work through it with your peers.
Taking a break every once in a while , and going out is very importaant , whether it's going out to exercise or having an outing with your friends, it's important to take breaks so your brain can recover.
